Profile

Dr. Tsivgoulis combines a background in physical medicine and rehabilitation with a distinct clinical orientation towards aesthetic restoration and regenerative medicine.

Focus Areas

Three core areas define his clinical and educational work.

Physical medicine and rehabilitation

Specialist in Physical Medicine and Rehabilitation with a clinical focus on neurological rehabilitation, spasticity management, and functional recovery protocols.

Aesthetic rehabilitation

Special interest in the aesthetic rehabilitation of neurological patients, combining functional restoration with injectable and regenerative approaches to facial and body appearance.

Injectables and regenerative practice

Trainer and key opinion leader for injectable products including Botox, Dysport, Pluryal, and Jalupro. International trainer and speaker for Burgeon x Vivacy, with clinical activity across anti-aging and regenerative aesthetic protocols.
